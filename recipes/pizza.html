Egg Dog

Description

Pizza, that elusive delicacy no one's ever heard of, finally has a recipe page — because obviously bread, sauce, and cheese needed formal documentation. Revered as the height of culinary complexity, it requires advanced skills like “calling for delivery” or “opening the oven door.” Historians debate whether pizza is actually food or just a socially acceptable excuse to stack random leftovers on carbs, while scientists confirm its primary function is burning the roof of your mouth on the first bite. Of course, it's also basically a salad — tomato sauce counts as a vegetable, right? — and pepperoni is the kind of emotional support meat we can all get behind

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Marinara sauce
  • Assorted cheeses
  • Pepperoni
  • Pinch of salt

Directions

Step 1

Spread the pizza dough out by rolling it and tossing it. Use flour to prevent sticking to hands. Then add marinara, cheese, and toppings as desired.

Step 2

Pre heat an oven to 425 and bake on a pizza pan for 12-18 minutes. Remove when cheese starts to turn golden brown.
