Lasagna

Description

What is there to say. Its lasagna. Its a homage to heart attacks. But for real, we all love it. We all crave it. The great poet, Garfield, once said, "Lasagna? I love lasagna."

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Cheese
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Egg
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings

Directions

Step 1

Make the meat sauce. Cook the noodles, then make the ricotta mixture. Layer the lasagna, and then cover with foil. Be sure to let rest before baking.

Step 2

The assembled lasagna should bake for about 50 minutes in an oven set to 350 degrees F. Cover it with foil for the first 25 minutes, then let it cook uncovered to finish. Also let it sit for at least 15 minutes before cutting.
