Egg Dog

Description

The Egg Dog is a classic American dish. While no one knows where it first came from, its regarded as perhaps the GOAT of food. Ancient philosophers fortold of a dish that would bring about a great change. The chosen food. There are many that speculate that this is it.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 tbsp butter
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 hot dog
  • 1/4 tsp kosher salt
  • 1/8 tsp freshly ground black pepper

Directions

Step 1

Melt butter in a small nonstick skillet over medium heat. Slice hot dog, and cook until browned on all sides. Turn heat down to medium low.

Step 2

Whisk eggs with salt and pepper unti well blended and pour over hot dogs. Cook, stirring constantly until eggs are set. Serve immediately.
